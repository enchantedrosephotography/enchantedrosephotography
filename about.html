Behind the Lens

Where emotion, storytelling, and artistry meet.

Hi, I’m the heart behind Enchanted Rose Photography. I believe photographs should feel something — not just document a moment, but preserve emotion, connection, and atmosphere.

My style is romantic, cinematic, and intentionally guided. Whether I’m photographing a wedding day, a glamour session, or a quiet family moment, my goal is always the same: to create images that feel timeless and deeply personal.

I specialize in helping clients feel comfortable and confident in front of the camera. You don’t need to know how to pose — I’ll guide you every step of the way with care and intention.

Based in Linden, Tennessee, I proudly serve surrounding areas and love traveling for meaningful stories. Every session is approached with artistry, patience, and respect for your vision.
