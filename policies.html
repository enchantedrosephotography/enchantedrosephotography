Policies & Disclaimer

Booking & Payment

  • A 30% non-refundable booking retainer is required to reserve your date.
  • The remaining balance is due on the session date.
  • Your date is not held until the retainer is paid.

No Refund Policy

All payments are non-refundable due to time reserved, preparation, and professional services provided.

Rescheduling & Weather

  • Outdoor sessions may be rescheduled due to unsafe weather.
  • Reschedules are subject to availability.
  • No-shows or cancellations forfeit all payments.

Artistic Style

By booking, you acknowledge that you have reviewed my portfolio and are hiring me for my creative style and editing approach.

Delivery

Final images are delivered digitally via online gallery. Clients are responsible for downloading and backing up files.
