Booking + payments

These policies protect your date and keep everything organized.

Retainer

A 30% non-refundable retainer is required to secure your date. Your session is not booked until the retainer is paid.

Remaining balance

The remaining balance is due on the session date (or by the due date listed on your invoice).

Cancellations

If you cancel, the retainer is not refunded. If you need to reschedule, see the rescheduling policy below.

Rescheduling

Life happens. If you need to reschedule, please give as much notice as possible. Reschedules are based on availability and may require a rescheduling fee in certain cases.

Weather

If weather is unsafe or would ruin the look of the session, we’ll reschedule. Cloudy/moody weather can be beautiful — we’ll decide together.

Editing + delivery

All delivered images are professionally edited in my signature style. I do not deliver unedited RAW files.

Questions before booking?

Send your date + session type and I’ll walk you through the next steps.
